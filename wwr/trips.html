Peaking at a whopping 12 kilometers, Big Man's Bluff is the longest trip option we offer. This river path is a customer favorite when it comes to the beautiful views of cliff's edges, the crystal clear water, and the impressive evergreens which line the riverbank.
The 8K Adventure is a great course for beginner rafters as it has the calmest waters of all our offered trips. That isn't to say there are not any whitewater areas! Part of the fun of rafting is being able to experience turbulent waters in a safe way. This trip has great scenery and the surrounding land is packed with history.
Backwash River is a course for those who desire a true river rafting challenge. Coming in at our most difficult course, this path contains over 3 kilometers of gruelling, rocky whitewater rapids. Be sure to bring your best paddling game if you decide to take on the challenge.
