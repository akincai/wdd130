Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to offer everyone from beginners to seasoned rafters access to our top of the line gear, professionally trained guides, and an unforgetable experience for you and your loved ones.

Audience

We provide a range of goods and services suited for inexperienced rafters, hobbyists, and professionals.
Q: Why should I consider getting rafting equipment if I'm only going on a one-time rafting trip?
A: We maintain top-of-the-line equipment which can be rented for day trips at low-end prices.

Q: What benefits come with hiring a rafting guide for my trip?
A: Our guides are CPR and First Aid certified with training in River Rescue. In addition, they have a vast knowledge of local scenery and the vast history of the region to share and broaden your experience.

Q: I want to know about various routes I can take for my trip. Where can I find information that describes route options?
A: You can visit our Trips page, where you can find out more about the various experience packages we offer.
