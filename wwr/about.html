History

We've been coasting along our energetic rivers since 1572. Our founder, John C. Apsize truly instilled his goal into this company: To allow anyone to easily book online, affordable rafting trips - even on tablet devices. Since our founding, we've set out on over 47,000 unique rafting trips with an impressive 95% success rate.
